AgriSense

Soil Health Analysis & Crop Type Identification System

Problem Statement

Farmers often lack clarity about soil nutrient composition, making it difficult to understand what type of crops the soil naturally supports. This results in poor crop planning and inefficient fertilizer usage.

Proposed Solution

AgriSense analyzes Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium (NPK) values to evaluate soil health, visualize nutrient distribution, and identify the crop type that the soil naturally corresponds to.
