Smart Agriculture – Interactive Soil Analysis

NPK-based decision support for better crop productivity

Problem Statement

Farmers often apply fertilizers without knowing the actual nutrient condition of soil, leading to wastage, reduced yield, and soil damage. A simple and interactive system is required to instantly analyze soil health using NPK values.

Solution Overview

This web-based tool analyzes Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium values obtained from sensors or lab tests and dynamically evaluates soil health, nutrient balance, and fertilizer recommendations.
