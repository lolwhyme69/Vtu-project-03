Smart Agriculture – Soil Health Analysis

Using NPK values to understand soil condition and improve crop yield

Problem Statement

In agriculture, farmers often apply fertilizers without knowing the exact nutrient condition of their soil. This leads to nutrient imbalance, reduced crop yield, increased cost, and long-term soil degradation. Traditional soil testing methods are time-consuming and not easily understandable for quick decision-making.

Proposed Solution

This system analyzes soil health using Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K) values. By entering NPK data obtained from a sensor or lab test, the system instantly determines the soil condition and provides a clear interpretation, helping farmers take informed fertilizer and crop decisions.

NPK Soil Analyzer

Benefits

  • Quick understanding of soil nutrient status
  • Prevents overuse or underuse of fertilizers
  • Improves crop productivity
  • Simple and farmer-friendly interpretation
